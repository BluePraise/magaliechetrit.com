How to become a WordPress Contributor - advice on how I help on MakeWordPress.
Why
I joined the accessibility team, because I believe the internet is close to becoming a safety need on Mazlow's Pyramid. It's very important that everyone can not just access web pages, but also join and contribute.
How
- Create a profile on WP.org - there you can find a selection of teams or topics you may find interesting. It may seem cool to just join "Core" or "UX", which it definitely is. These groups have a lot of help so if you want to really join in fast I find joining the "smaller" teams to familiarize yourself. For example, let's say you'd join the "Photos" team, you'd be able to learn so much about media in web applications. Whichever team you end up with, join the meetings frequently (all of them). No need to prove yourself, the team is already happy to see you. Introduce yourself and pipe in on a matter that you have an opinion on.

## Tips
- ### Get Organized
Once you decide on a group or a two groups, put their meeting schedule in your calendar. On this page you can even get an RSS feed and add meetings to your digital calendar.
